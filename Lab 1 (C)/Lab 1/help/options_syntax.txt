		Initialization file syntax

At first comes property name, then without whitespaces, after equal sign, property value.
Rules always must end with semicolon.

Example:
SHOW_STUDENT_INFO=N;
SHOW_EMAIL=N;
SHOW_MENU=N;
SHOW_HELP=Y;
RUN_ENCRYPTING=Y;
INPUT_FILENAME=input.txt;
OUTPUT_FILENAME=output.txt;
KEY_FILENAME=key.txt;
